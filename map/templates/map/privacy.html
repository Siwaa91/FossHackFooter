Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy of Software Freedom Law Center, India (SFLC.in) is aimed at protecting the privacy of users visiting our websites.

Websites here refer to the parent website; SFLC.IN, Internet Shutdowns, https://security.sflc.in,well as https://privacy.sflc.in,

‘SFLC.in’; ‘US’ and ‘WE’ as mentioned herein referred to the organisation SFLC.in, a society registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and includes its Board of Directors, Management, staff, counsels, interns, volunteers, consultants and other agents.

This Policy covers the collection, storage, security, processing and disclosure of personal data.



Information Collected


Information is collected by us to analyze the usage of the website by the users visiting it and consequently enhance user experience based on the information collected.

The IP address of the user visiting our websites is anonymized to ensure that no individual can be identified.

The following information is collected -

  • The Region
  • User browser and Browser Engine
  • Operating system and Screen resolution
  • The data and time when the websites were accessed.

The above information is collected to -

  • Optimize user experience by ensuring compatibility of the browsers as well as the Operating systems with our website.
  • To understand how our websites and services are being used.

When you communicate with us through our site, we may collect your name, email address, postal address, and other personal information.



Third Party Disclosure


Information collected by us is not revealed to any third party except as compelled by Law. In the case of signature campaigns conducted by the organization, we may share name and personal information of the persons who have signed on to the campaign to the recipients of the letters.



Third Party Applications


The following third party applications are used by us to optimize user experience - Social media websites like Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, Mastodon and YouTube. We also use services like MapBox Leaflet. We may use EventBrite for managing our events and Kutt.it, a URL shortening open source service. We also use the services of Matamo, you can read their privacy policy here. third party websites are governed by their own privacy policies and users should refer to them. No user information is shared by us to these third party apps.



Storage


The information collected is stored on our own servers as well as on third party cloud servers that are used by us. We also store data in encrypted hard drives.

The data is directly accessible by the Technology team of the organization. A written request can be made by any other member of the organization for access to data which can be accepted and denied by the technology team.



Security


All the data that is collected by us is encrypted and stored. Utmost caution is used while handling any third party data. The data is stored for archival purposes.



Links to Other Websites


Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us. If You click on a third party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.



Changes to this Privacy Policy


We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time.
